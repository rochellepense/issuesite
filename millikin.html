Millikin University




Millikin University is considered to be a safe space for many minority groups. However, despite this, there are several groups that feel some injustice towards them. One example of this is the LGBTQ+ community on campus. While Millikin University expresses its support for diversity, there are some issues that the students are facing.





Discrimination based on people's own point of view



A lot of students till believe themselves to be the only ones who are right. They are not very open-minded and tend to discount anyone whose opinion is different from theirs.




Homophobia



I feel like a lot of the people on the sports floor in Dolson are insanely homophobic. I live on that floor and I have heard so many not okay things said. It worries me that if they knew I was gay, what would they say or do? It makes me feel on edge




Searching for drama



Instead of just observing things happening and fixing them, a lot of people seem to be purposefully looking for something to be upset over

Slurs used against people

Some of the fraternities were caught using slurs last year and it really put me off joining Greek Life.
